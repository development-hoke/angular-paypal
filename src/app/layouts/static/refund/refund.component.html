FAQ – Frequently Asked Questions about refunds

How long does it take to receive the refund?

Compufy will grant the refund as soon as it is requested. We’ll have a check/money order sent to you by mail within 5 days, or you’ll see the refund on your credit card statement with the next billing cycle. If there are no exceptional mail delays, you should expect to receive your refund within 20 days from your request.

What happens if I decide to upgrade within 30 days, since I bought one of your products? Do I need to pay the full upgrade price?

If you decide to upgrade your product to a higher edition within 30 days since purchase, you’ll only need to pay the difference in price from the edition you bought to the edition you’d like to upgrade to. Just email at info@Compufytechnologyllc.com with your receipt details and we will gladly help you upgrade.

What if I ask for a refund and later I want to purchase one of Compufy’s products?

We will gladly accept you again as a customer. If you decide to return a product, please allow 30 days for the bill cycle to archive your previous purchase and we’ll welcome you on-board (again).

Exactly how much will I get back if I request a refund?

You will get a check/money order or credit card refund for the partially amount we charged as we mentioned in the invoice you received.

Are there any non-refundable fees?

There may be some transaction fees charged by your credit card or bank we will not reimburse. We will, however, issue a check/money order or credit card refund for the full purchase amount.
